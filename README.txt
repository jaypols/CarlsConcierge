
  Carl's Concierge

--- Playable on Unity
https://play.unity.com/mg/other/carl-s-concierge


--- About
Operating at the Ritz-Carl, the concierge is expected to provide the upmost quality for its guests as Carl navigates through the hotel in this 2D Platformer prototype. 


--- Goal of this Game
Utilize Carl's triple jump to deliver luggage to every room before the timer runs out. A successful luggage delivery counts as a point towards the score.


--- How To Play
Left-Arrow : Move Carl Left
Right-Arrow : Move Carl Right 
Up-Arrow : Jump with Carl
